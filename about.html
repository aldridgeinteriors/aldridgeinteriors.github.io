Great designs begin with close designer-client collaboration. We start with an initial meeting to understand your needs, vision, and space. We then spend time developing your design, doing whatever we can to help you visualize your project. We will stick with you through each step of the design process to make sure everything runs smoothly, making appropriate changes until the design is complete, and you are satisfied. Our goal is to develop lasting relationships and have our clients come back to us for all their design needs.
We keep the environment's and our client's best interest in mind during all our projects. We research and choose products that are environmentally friendly and have a long lifecycle. We also put care into making sure the products we specify help to create healthy spaces for our clients.
Kayla earned her Bachelor's degree in studio art from Saint Mary's College, then went on to receive her degree in interior design. She is a LEED Green Associate who focuses greatly on sustainability and environmentally friendly designs. Kayla has been working in the industry for over three years, with an emphasis on classic-contemporary and modern design styles. Kayla's main goal is always to create a healthy and inspiring design that reflects her clients.
